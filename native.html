Project Overview

A local animal adoption organisation, needed a new website that would showcase their adoptable pets and streamline the adoption process. They wanted a solution that could be easily maintained by their staff while providing a warm, engaging experience for potential adopters.

The Challenge

The main challenges for this project were:

  • Creating an emotional connection between visitors and adoptable pets
  • Building a template system that non-technical staff could easily update
  • Balancing heartwarming content with professional organization

My Approach

I began with research into successful animal adoption to understand their needs and pain points.

Based on these insights, I created a design strategy that emphasised large, high-quality images of animals, engaging storytelling elements, and clear calls-to-action for the adoption process.

I developed a flexible template system with modular components that would allow staff to easily update animal profiles while maintaining design consistency. The UI was crafted to be warm and inviting while still professional, using a colour palette inspired by nature and animal-friendly themes.

Deliverables

Website Design

Website design with special attention to showcasing animal photos effectively.

Template System

Customizable templates for animal profiles, success stories, and event announcements.

Key Features

  • Animal Profile Cards: Visually engaging cards showing key information at a glance

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the power of emotional design in driving user engagement. By focusing on compelling animal photography and heartfelt storytelling, we created a connection that significantly increased visitor engagement.

I also gained valuable experience in designing for non-technical users. Creating a template system that balanced flexibility with design guardrails required careful consideration of the staff's technical abilities and workflow needs.

Perhaps most importantly, this project demonstrated how thoughtful UI design can have a real-world impact - in this case, helping more animals find loving homes.
