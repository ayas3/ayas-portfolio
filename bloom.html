Project Overview

SouthSide Bloom is a local charity organisation focused on community development and youth education programs. I did redesign their navigation with a focus on improving user experience.

The Challenge

The main challenges for this project were:

  • Creating a more intuitive navigation structure
  • Ensuring accessibility for all users

My Approach

I began with a thorough analysis of the existing website, identifying pain points and opportunities for improvement.

After gathering insights, I created wireframes focusing on a clean, intuitive layout that prioritized the most important content and actions. I developed a refreshed visual style that maintained the organization's brand identity while giving it a more modern, professional appearance.

Deliverables

Simplified user experience

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of understanding an organization's mission and their users' needs before diving into design. Working with a charity emphasised the need for clear communication and intuitive interfaces that can be used by people of varying technical abilities.
